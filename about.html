About Me

Get to know the person behind the projects

Hi! I'm Victoria Laguerre, and I'm a Manufacturing and Design Engineering major at Northwestern University.

Since I took my first woodworking class in 7th grade, I have come to always ask myself this question when it comes to design...

...who am I designing for?

It's trivial. I know.

But trivial doesn't equal unimportant.

Whether it's making something for a client, a professor, or even my mom, the USER and their NEEDS is the most important part of the design thinking process for me.

Whenever I'm developing a product to solve a problem, or devising a service to try and improve people's lives...

...I am driven by an urge to better my society.

And so, when I design, I do it by listening, iterating, and refining the user experience every step of the way.

As that is what creates long-lasting benefits.

And the great thing about this process, it truly is long-lasting.

Innovation never dwindles, and creativity never dies.

I can't wait to design for you!
